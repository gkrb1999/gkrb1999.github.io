I am currently pursuing a Masters in Aeronautics and Astronautics at the University of Washington, Seattle with a focus on Fluid Dynamics. I completed my undergraduate with a Bachelor of Science in Aerospace Engineering from the Indian Institute of Aeronautical Engineering, Dehradun. My career interests lie in Mechanical design engineering, Product design engineer, CAE Engineer, CFD Engineer, Thermal engineer, Fluid dynamics and Aerodynamics roles.

Before starting my master's program, I worked for startup companies focusing on CAD designing and analysis for developing new products like rocket engines and aircraft models. Additionally, I am currently working on an Aerodynamics research project with Boeing as part of my master's degree thesis requirements to analyze stall cells during high lift conditions.
